# Additional Market Research Archive

This archive holds serious market studies that are not promoted to the current
flagship portfolio lines.

The point is not to hide them. They show two different kinds of research
discipline:

| Research Line | What Worked | Why It Is Archived |
|---|---|---|
| [Futures CTA](futures-cta/README.md) | Low-correlation trend/carry structure, OOS pooling effect, clean capital-ladder evidence | Personal capital granularity: whole-lot futures only became usable around CNY 3m+ |
| [A-share Small-cap](a-share-small-cap/README.md) | Strong gross small-cap return engine | Drawdown, sell-fail, suspension/no-bar, and long-only beta risk did not fit the core book |

These pages are secondary evidence beside the current public portfolio:

- Convertible Bonds and Crypto remain the flagship research lines.
- ETF Stabilizer remains a supporting allocation sleeve.
- CB + ETF Bridge remains portfolio-construction evidence.
- Additional Market Research is an archive of market-fit judgments, not a queue
  of strategies waiting to be deployed.

## Reader Path

1. Start with the [Portfolio Map](../../docs/portfolio-map.md) for the full role map.
2. Read [Futures CTA](futures-cta/README.md) for a clean example of "good strategy logic, wrong capital fit."
3. Read [A-share Small-cap](a-share-small-cap/README.md) for a clean example of "strong gross return, unsuitable core risk structure."
4. Use the [Evidence Index](../../docs/evidence-index.md) to trace public claims to reports, tables, figures, and code anchors.

## Publication Boundary

This archive includes selected scripts, reports, summary tables, and figures.
It intentionally excludes raw data, licensed data, data caches, API credentials,
`.env` files, broker files, live orders, and full stock-level event dumps.
